What makes us stand out


We’re passionate about delivering hot, great tasting, freshly handcrafted pizzas to customers. This relentless focus has helped us become the UK’s leading pizza brand, in addition to being a major player in the Republic of Ireland and several other markets, including Iceland and Norway. Since opening the first Pizza House store in the UK in 1985, we now have over 1,000 stores across the country and more than 35,000 team members. Last year, we sold almost 90 million freshly handcrafted pizzas, including over 7 million of the UK’s favourite, Pepperoni Passion. We’re committed to offering choice, and with thousands of combinations on our menu, customers have plenty of options. Our pizzas are made with great quality sourced ingredients, such as fresh creamy 100% mozzarella, vine-ripened tomato sauce, and our signature fresh dough.
